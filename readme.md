## ğŸ® Gameplay Tips

### Mars Colony Builder Tips:
- **Start with solar panels** to generate energy
- **Build habitats** to increase population
- **Connect with roads** for aesthetics
- **Balance resources** - habitats consume food, water, and energy
- **Watch for shortages** - warnings appear when resources are low
- **Spaceports are expensive** but generate lots of credits

### Space Shooter Tips:

- **Rewind saves lives** - use it when overwhelmed
- **Collect shields** - they protect from damage
- **Shoot asteroids** for points
- **Boss appears at level 3** - save your abilities
